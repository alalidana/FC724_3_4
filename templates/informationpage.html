Reasons for collecting data on student progress

  1. Understanding the effectiveness of current teaching methods.
  2. GIC will be able to identify areas that need improvement in the curriculum.
  3. Assessing student satisfaction with the learning environment and make changes if needed.
  4. Tracking student progress over time and identify trends.
  5. Gathering feedback for the development of future programs and initiatives.
  6. Identifying any barriers to learning that students may be facing.
  7. To support decision-making processes related to academic planning.
